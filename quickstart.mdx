# Create your first crawl

Follow these steps to create your own custom Star Wars opening crawl video.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the crawl creator">
    Navigate to the [Star Wars Crawl Creator](https://gostargazer.com/star-wars-crawl/) web form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in the opening text">
    Enter your **Start Text** - this is the text that appears before the title (e.g., "A long time ago, in a galaxy far far away...").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the title">
    Set up your title sequence:
    - **Title Row 1**: The first line of the title (e.g., "STAR")
    - **Title Row 2**: The second line of the title (e.g., "WARS")
    - **Episode Title**: Your episode designation (e.g., "Episode IV: A New Hope")
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add crawl content">
    Write your crawl text:
    - **Crawl Heading**: The heading that appears at the start of the crawl text
    - **Crawl Text**: The main text that scrolls up the screen
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter delivery details">
    Provide your **Email Address** where the video will be delivered.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose resolution">
    Select your preferred video quality:
    - **SD**: Standard definition
    - **HD**: High definition
    - **4K**: Ultra high definition
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit and wait">
    Submit the form and check your email. Your video will be generated and delivered within a few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form fields explained

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start Text">
    The opening text that appears before the title card. The classic text is "A long time ago, in a galaxy far far away..." but you can customize this to anything you want.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Title Row 1 & Title Row 2">
    These two fields create the iconic split title. The original uses "STAR" and "WARS" but you can use any text to create your own title sequence.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Episode Title">
    The episode designation that appears after the main title, such as "Episode IV" or your own custom episode number and subtitle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Crawl Heading">
    The heading text that appears at the beginning of the crawling text sequence. This sets up the story context.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Crawl Text">
    The main body of text that scrolls up the screen. This is where you tell your story. The text will crawl into the starfield just like the opening of Star Wars films.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email Address">
    Your email address where the generated video will be delivered. Make sure this is correct as you won't be able to retrieve your video otherwise.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Resolution">
    Choose the quality of your output video:
    - **SD**: Good for web sharing and smaller file sizes
    - **HD**: Best for most uses, balances quality and file size
    - **4K**: Highest quality for professional projects
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What to expect

<Note>
  After submitting your form, the video generation process begins automatically. Your custom Star Wars crawl video will be delivered to your email address **within a few minutes**.
</Note>
