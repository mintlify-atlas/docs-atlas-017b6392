## General questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to receive my video?">
    Your custom Star Wars crawl video is typically generated and delivered to your email within a few minutes after submitting the form. During peak times, delivery may take up to 15 minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What email address should I use?">
    Use an email address you check regularly. Make sure to enter it correctly when filling out the form, as this is where your generated video will be sent. We recommend using a personal email rather than a temporary or disposable email address.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What video resolutions are available?">
    You can choose from three resolution options:
    - **SD (Standard Definition)** - Best for quick previews and social media
    - **HD (High Definition)** - Great for most uses and presentations
    - **4K (Ultra HD)** - Maximum quality for professional projects and large screens
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What video format will I receive?">
    The video is delivered in a standard MP4 format, which is compatible with virtually all video players, editing software, and platforms including YouTube, Vimeo, and social media.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a cost to create a crawl video?">
    Check the [Star Wars Crawl Creator website](https://gostargazer.com/star-wars-crawl/) for current pricing information and any available free options.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customization

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What can I customize in my crawl?">
    You can customize the following elements:
    - **Start Text** - The opening line (default: "A long time ago, in a galaxy far far away...")
    - **Title Row 1** - First part of the title (e.g., "STAR")
    - **Title Row 2** - Second part of the title (e.g., "WARS")
    - **Episode Title** - Your episode number or name
    - **Crawl Heading** - The main heading of your crawl text
    - **Crawl Text** - The main story text that scrolls up the screen
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long can my crawl text be?">
    While there's no strict character limit, keep in mind that very long text will result in a longer video. For the best viewing experience, aim for 200-400 words - similar to the length of actual Star Wars opening crawls.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use special characters or emojis?">
    Standard text characters, punctuation, and line breaks are supported. However, emojis and special Unicode characters may not render correctly. Stick to standard letters, numbers, and punctuation for best results.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change the music or sound effects?">
    The tool uses the classic Star Wars theme and sound effects. Custom audio tracks are not currently supported to maintain the authentic Star Wars experience.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical requirements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to install any software?">
    No installation required. The Star Wars Crawl Creator is entirely web-based. Simply visit the website, fill out the form, and receive your video via email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What browsers are supported?">
    The form works with all modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Make sure your browser is up to date for the best experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create videos on mobile devices?">
    Yes, you can fill out the form and submit your crawl from any device with a web browser, including smartphones and tablets.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
